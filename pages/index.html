How does it work?

TeamUP is a FREE community-driven platform for finding collaborations for your product idea, side, or Hackathon project. We believe all this can be done in three easy steps.

1

Create a free account

Continue with Google and gain access to a free account on the platform.

2

Explore & create Ads

Create Ads about your Project, Hackathon, or Product Idea for other users in the community to see.

3

Onboard & Collaborate

Interested collaborators would apply, and you can then redirect them to your team-group chat.
